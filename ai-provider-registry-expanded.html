§ I Validation Flags — Source Material Discrepancies
⚠ Invalidated
FLAG-01 · xAI Corp · PBC Status
Source material describes xAI as a Nevada Benefit Corporation. INVALIDATED. Public records confirm xAI terminated PBC status on May 9, 2024 via charter amendment. Further: as of May 6, 2026, xAI was folded into SpaceX as the "SpaceXAI" division. The Nevada corp shell (X.AI Corp.) was the merger vehicle; Grok and X social platform are now SpaceX assets. xAI has misrepresented PBC status in federal court filings (documented by LASST, Aug 2025).
⚑ Superseded
FLAG-02 · OpenAI · Corporate Structure
Source lists "OpenAI, Inc. (501c3)" as primary entity. SUPERSEDED. As of October 28, 2025 at 8:07 AM EDT, the Delaware Division of Corporations approved OPENAI GROUP PBC (30-page COI). OpenAI Inc. became the OpenAI Foundation (nonprofit, retains 26% equity + control rights). The for-profit LLC transitioned to OpenAI Group PBC. CIK 0001986421 maps to the new PBC entity. Microsoft holds 27% equity. Transition required consent of Delaware and California AGs.
⚑ Superseded
FLAG-03 · Grok / xAI · Parent Entity
Source lists Grok's parent as "xAI Corp / X Holdings Corp." SUPERSEDED. X Corp merged into xAI (March 28, 2025, all-stock at $33B valuation). Then xAI merged into SpaceX (May 6, 2026), forming the SpaceXAI division. Current parent entity: Space Exploration Technologies Corp. (SpaceX). Grok and X are now SpaceX products.

FLAG-06 · Fal AI · Jurisdiction Anomaly
Source lists Fal AI as incorporated in Anguilla (British Overseas Territory) with a California USA operational presence. This is a materially unusual offshore/onshore split for an AI inference company. Anguilla incorporation is used for IP holding and offshore capital structures. The "Series D Entity / Private" designation suggests the Delaware entity filed under a different structure. Warrants further monitoring.
● Note
FLAG-07 · OpenRouter · Dual Entity Architecture
Source identifies both "OpenRouter LLC" (Delaware, State File 7293819) and "RAMSGen Ltd" (England & Wales, CRN 16458298) as overlapping entities for the same operation. This is a legitimate dual-jurisdiction structure. The UK entity (RAMSGen Ltd) serves as EU/UK operations vehicle; the Delaware LLC is the US contracting entity. These are separate registered legal entities sharing operational function — standard liability compartmentalization.
● Note
FLAG-08 · DeepSeek · Irish Entity
Source lists ICP number 2023025841 (China) and IE01278123 (Ireland). The Irish entity "Deepseek AI Limited" (CRN IE01278123) is the EU-facing legal vehicle, likely established for GDPR compliance and as an EEA data controller under the AI Act. This creates a tri-jurisdictional architecture: China (ICP/state data access) → Ireland (EU GDPR/AI Act) → Global API surface. The High-Flyer Capital Management parent fund (Liang Wenjie) has direct PRC state-adjacent relationships.
● Note
FLAG-09 · Baseten · Dual Registry Numbers
Two different identifiers appear across source sections for Baseten: Delaware State File 7727394 (Secretary of State) and CIK 0001865393 (SEC/EDGAR filer). Both can be valid simultaneously — the state file is the SOC domestic number; the CIK is the SEC registration number for capital markets filings. No conflict, but the discrepancy between source sections suggests the two were pulled from different registry sources.
● Note

CIK 0000796343 is consistent and unchanged across the name change. The Firefly product is a division of Adobe Inc., not a separately incorporated entity.
